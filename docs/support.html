Frequently Asked Questions

The metronome stops when I lock my screen

Enable "Background Play" in Settings. On Android, this requires notification permission so the app can run as a foreground service.

How do I use tap tempo?

Tap the "Tap" button repeatedly at your desired tempo. After two or more taps, the BPM adjusts to match your rhythm.

What is gradual tempo?

Gradual tempo slowly increases the BPM over time during practice. Set a start BPM, end BPM, and how many bars between each increase. Great for building speed.

Can I change the click sound?

Yes. Go to Settings and choose between Wood, Click, or Classic sounds.
