Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Metronome: Feel the Beat! is designed with your privacy in mind. The app works entirely offline and does not require an account or personal information to use.

Data We Collect

The app uses the following third-party services on Android:

  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. This includes device type, OS version, and stack traces. No personal data is included.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous usage data such as session duration and screen views. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve it.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring — collects anonymous performance metrics to help us optimize the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your settings (tempo, sound, theme, practice time) are stored locally on your device using DataStore. This data never leaves your device.

No Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We do not require registration, login, or any personal details.

No Ads

The app contains no advertisements and no ad tracking.

Third-Party Services

Firebase services are provided by Google. You can review their privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children. The anonymous crash and analytics data collected by Firebase does not include personal information from any user.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at merkostdev+metronome@gmail.com.
