## Google Play Full Description

Metronome is a clean, reliable metronome app designed for musicians of all levels.

Features:
- Precise audio engine with low-latency playback
- Tap tempo for quick BPM detection
- Gradual tempo increase for practice sessions
- Multiple time signatures
- Selectable click sounds
- Visual beat indicator with animated ball
- Timer mode for timed practice
- Keeps playing with screen off
- Light and dark themes with multiple color schemes
- No ads, no internet required

Built with care for musicians who need a metronome that just works.
